# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Professionals, developers, students, and content creators who use 2+ AI platforms daily (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, Grok, Llama). They are power users frustrated by re-writing prompts from scratch, losing prompt history, and context-switching between AI interfaces. Primary job: save, find, and reuse high-quality prompts without leaving their current tool.

## Product Purpose

PromptVault Pro is the universal prompt layer: a Chrome Extension + React web dashboard + FastAPI backend that saves, organizes, tags, versions, and AI-refines prompts across every major AI platform. A floating overlay injects into any AI chat interface. Secondary surface: a prompt marketplace where users can buy and sell prompts. Success = user opens an AI interface and their best prompts are already there, refined and ready.

## Brand Personality

Precise, professional, trusted.

Voice: confident without being loud. Speaks to experts without condescending to newcomers. The tool is the expert; the user feels more capable, not tutored.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS cream: Notion-white, endless pastel cards, soft meaningless gradients
- Enterprise grey: SAP/Oracle density, feels like filing a form
- Consumer cute: Duolingo-rounded, bubbly app-store pastels, excessive friendliness

## Design Principles

1. **Earn invisibility.** The tool should disappear into the user's workflow. No choreography, no ceremony. The prompt appears; the distraction doesn't.
2. **Show capability, don't claim it.** Every screen should demonstrate what the product does rather than describe it. The auth page's left panel is a sales moment; make it work.
3. **Precision over decoration.** Tight typography, deliberate color, exact spacing. Ornament that doesn't carry meaning is noise.
4. **Trust is cumulative.** Social proof, privacy signals, and brand consistency are not nice-to-haves. They compound at every interaction.
5. **Respect the expert.** Target users are not being onboarded to AI — they are already heavy AI users. The UI should match their fluency.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AAA (7:1 contrast minimum for text). Keyboard navigation required throughout. Full screen reader support. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.
